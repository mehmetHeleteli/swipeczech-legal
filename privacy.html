Privacy Policy for SwipeLanguage

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

SwipeLanguage ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Data We Collect

Information You Provide

  • GameCenter ID: Your GameCenter profile name and ID for displaying on leaderboards (optional)
  • Purchase Information: Transaction data for in-app purchases (processed by Apple)
  • Language Preferences: Your selected learning language pairs and settings

Automatically Collected Data

  • Learning Progress: Words learned, scores, accuracy, streaks, and progress (stored locally on your device)
  • GameCenter Data: Your scores, achievements, and leaderboard rankings (stored by Apple GameCenter)
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, app version
  • Advertising Data (Free Version Only):
    • Advertising Identifier (IDFA) - for showing personalized ads
    • Device identifiers
    • IP address
    • Usage data and app interaction
    • Ad impressions and clicks
    • This data is collected by Google AdMob, not by us
  • App Usage: Features used, crashes and errors (for app improvement)
  • Language Learning Data: Which languages you're learning, difficulty preferences (stored locally)

What We Don't Collect

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or contact information
  • No Account Required: The app works without registration or login
  • No Location Data: We don't track your physical location
  • No Cloud Storage: Your learning data stays on your device only
  • Premium Users: We don't collect advertising data if you purchase Remove Ads or Premium subscription

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide App Functionality: Track your learning progress across multiple languages locally on your device
  • Display Leaderboards: Show your ranking and scores on Apple GameCenter leaderboards (if you're signed in)
  • Personalize Experience: Adapt difficulty and show relevant content based on your progress
  • Show Advertisements: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob (free version only)
  • Improve the App: Fix bugs, analyze usage patterns, and add features
  • Process Purchases: Handle in-app purchases and subscriptions through Apple

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Apple GameCenter (Optional)

For leaderboards and achievements. If you sign in to GameCenter, your profile information and scores are stored by Apple.

  • GameCenter ID and profile name
  • Scores and achievements
  • Leaderboard rankings

View Apple GameCenter Privacy Policy

Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

For displaying advertisements to free users. AdMob collects:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Ad interaction data

View AdMob Privacy Policy

Apple StoreKit

For in-app purchases and subscriptions. Apple handles all payment processing securely. We don't store credit card information.

View Apple Privacy Policy

Important: If you purchase "Remove Ads" or a Premium subscription, AdMob is completely disabled and no advertising data is collected.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your learning progress is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage
  • GameCenter Storage: Leaderboard data and achievements are stored by Apple's GameCenter service
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not store your personal data on our servers
  • Security: Your data is protected by iOS security features including encryption
  • Data Control: You have complete control - delete the app to delete local data

Your Choices and Rights

Control GameCenter

  • Sign Out of GameCenter: iOS Settings → Game Center → Sign out
  • Disable GameCenter: iOS Settings → Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions → Game Center
  • Manage Leaderboard Visibility: GameCenter privacy settings control who sees your scores

Control Advertising Tracking

  • Disable Ad Tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Toggle off for SwipeLanguage
  • Reset Advertising ID: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Reset Identifier
  • Remove Ads Completely: Purchase "Remove Ads" ($0.99) or Premium subscription

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of data we have (contact us at mehmetheleteli@gmail.com)
  • Delete Your Data: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt Out of Tracking: Disable tracking in iOS settings
  • Port Your Data: Request your data in a portable format
To delete all your data:
  1. Delete the SwipeLanguage app from your device (removes all local learning progress)
  2. Disable tracking in iOS settings (stops future ad tracking)
  3. Contact us at mehmetheleteli@gmail.com if you have additional concerns

Children's Privacy

SwipeLanguage is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app does not require account creation or personal information from any user, including children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at mehmetheleteli@gmail.com.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app
  • GameCenter Data: Managed by Apple according to their retention policies (typically retained indefinitely unless you delete your GameCenter account)
  • Advertising Data: Managed by AdMob according to their retention policies (typically 30 days)
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Deletion Requests: Processed within 30 days of receiving your request
